Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

Go to the Settings tab and select "Reset Progress". This will clear all your statistics and session history.

Is this app affiliated with Amazon?

No, Certisimo SAA is an independent study resource created to help you prepare for the AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate certification. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Amazon Web Services (AWS).

How many questions are included?

The app includes 390+ practice questions across 6 test sets, covering all exam domains for the SAA-C03 exam.

Are the questions updated for the latest exam?

Yes! Our questions are updated for the latest SAA-C03 exam objectives (2024 version), covering all current exam domains and AWS services.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All questions and explanations are available offline. You can study anywhere without an internet connection.

What topics are covered?

EC2, S3, VPC, IAM, RDS, Aurora, DynamoDB, Lambda, CloudFront, Route 53, Auto Scaling, ELB, CloudWatch, CloudFormation, and all other Solutions Architect Associate exam domains.

How do Training and Test modes differ?

Training mode gives you instant feedback after each question. Test mode simulates the real exam with 50 questions in 2 hours and shows results only at the end.
